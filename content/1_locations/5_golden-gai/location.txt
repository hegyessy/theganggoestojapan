Title: Golden Gai

----

Description: A maze of tiny alleyways packed with over 200 micro-bars, each seating 5-10 people. Every bar has its own unique theme and character. Pure Tokyo magic. Some bars charge a cover (¥500-1000).

----

Category: bar
