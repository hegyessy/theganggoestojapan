Title: Meiji Shrine

----

Description: A peaceful Shinto shrine surrounded by a lush forest right in the heart of Tokyo. Dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken. The forested approach is stunning.

----

Category: shrine

----

Address: 1-1 Yoyogikamizonocho, Shibuya City, Tokyo 151-8557
