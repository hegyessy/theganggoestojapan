Title: Senso-ji Temple

----

Description: Tokyo's oldest temple, dating back to 645 AD. The iconic Kaminarimon gate and Nakamise shopping street leading up to it are unforgettable. Beautiful lit up at night too.

----

Category: shrine

----

Address: 2 Chome-3-1 Asakusa, Taito City, Tokyo 111-0032
